Steward X is a high-performance utility designed to keep you informed about your surroundings. It turns your device into a localized monitoring station for:* <b>Seismic Activity:</b> Detects vibrations and environmental shifts using onboard sensors(Magnitude 4.5+ incidents automatically detected).
*<b>Air Quality:</b> Monitors pollution levels (AQI) in your area and convert the AQI score to easily understandable language to keep your lungs safe. Live Air Quality and Safety telemetry is fetched from public APIs.* 
<b>Safety Alerts:</b> Provides awareness regarding local safety data and incidents.* 
<b>Privacy First:</b> No ads, no trackers, and no data selling. Just pure, open-source protection.
